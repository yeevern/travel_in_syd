Place to Visit in Sydney

Echo Point Lookout

Take a day trip to famous Echo Point lookout in Katoomba for stunning views of the Jamison Valley and the iconic Three Sisters, in the Blue Mountains.

Admission Type: Free

Scenic Skyway

Glide between cliff tops with panoramic views of World Heritage landscapes on the Scenic Skyway. This 720 metre journey provides the best views of Katoomba Falls, the Three Sisters, Mt Solitary and Jamison Valley.

Admission Type: Paid

Scenic Railway

Scenic Railway. Discover the thrill of a 52° (128%) incline in open terrain riding the steepest passenger railway in the world, the Scenic Railway.

Admission Type: Paid

Wentworth Falls Loop

A challenging walking track near Conservation Hut, Wentworth Pass loop offers lookouts, scenic views, and waterfalls near Wentworth Falls in Blue Mountains.

Admission Type: Free

Eagle Hawk Lookout

Located along Cliff Road just up past Scenic World, Eagle Hawk Lookout is quite spectacular. It gives you wonderful views down and across the valley.

Admission Type: Free

Grand Canyon Walking Track

Popular 6-km. loop track offering dramatic views of sandstone cliffs, waterfalls & rainforest.

Admission Type: Free
